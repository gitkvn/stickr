Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

TL;DR

Stickr uses Google sign-in for accounts, stores your files temporarily on Cloudflare R2 (24 hours for free, 7 days for Pro), transfers small files directly between devices via encrypted P2P, and never reads your file contents. We collect only what's needed to make the service work.

Your account

Stickr uses Google OAuth for authentication. When you sign in, we receive and store your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This information is used to create your Stickr profile and identify you in the service. We also generate a unique username for your profile URL.

Your account data is stored in a SQLite database on our server hosted on Railway. Session tokens are stored in secure, HTTP-only cookies.

Peer-to-peer file transfers

When you share files using the "Share Files" or "Join Room" features, Stickr establishes a direct peer-to-peer connection between browsers using WebRTC. Files under 100 MB travel directly from the sender's device to the recipient's device. Our server only facilitates the initial connection — it never sees, stores, or processes your file contents.

All P2P data is encrypted using DTLS (Datagram Transport Layer Security), which is built into WebRTC. Neither our signaling server nor any relay server can decrypt your data.

TURN relay

In approximately 10–20% of connections, a direct P2P connection isn't possible due to network restrictions (firewalls, symmetric NATs). In these cases, data is relayed through Cloudflare's TURN service. The relayed data remains encrypted — the relay cannot read your file contents. TURN credentials are ephemeral (short-lived) and generated fresh for each session.

Cloud-uploaded files (Get a Link, profile uploads)

When you use "Get a Link" or when someone sends you files via your upload link, those files are uploaded to Cloudflare R2 object storage. These files are:

— Stored temporarily: 24 hours on the free plan, 7 days on Pro

— Accessible only via a unique, unguessable download token

— Automatically deleted after expiry

— Not encrypted at the application level (Cloudflare R2 encrypts data at rest on their infrastructure)

We do not read, scan, or analyze the contents of uploaded files. File metadata (name, size, MIME type, upload time) is stored in our database to manage the service.

Pinned files

Pinned files on your profile are stored on Cloudflare R2 and do not expire. They are publicly downloadable by anyone who visits your profile page. You can remove pinned files at any time.

Your profile page

Your Stickr profile (getstickr.in/username) displays your name, profile picture, bio, social links, and pinned files. This information is publicly visible to anyone who visits your profile URL. You control what appears on your profile through the edit profile settings.

Upload links and inbox

When you generate an upload link, anyone with that link can send you files. These files are stored on Cloudflare R2 and appear in your inbox. Upload links expire after 24 hours. Files received via upload links follow the same expiry rules as other cloud-uploaded files.

What we store in our database

— Account information: Google ID, name, email, profile picture, username

— Profile data: bio, social links, business card info (if you set these up)

— Session tokens for authentication

— File metadata: filename, size, upload time, expiry time, download tokens

— Transfer records for usage tracking

— Plan status (free or Pro)

What we don't do

— We don't read or scan your file contents

— We don't use analytics or third-party tracking scripts

— We don't sell your data to anyone

— We don't show advertisements

— We don't share your data with third parties except as described (Google for auth, Cloudflare for storage/relay, Dodo Payments for billing)

Payments

Pro subscriptions are processed through Dodo Payments, which acts as our Merchant of Record. When you upgrade to Pro, your email and name are shared with Dodo Payments to create your subscription. Dodo Payments handles all payment processing, tax calculation, and billing. We store your Dodo customer ID and subscription status in our database. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details.

Data retention

— Cloud-uploaded files: automatically deleted after 24 hours (free) or 7 days (Pro)

— Pinned files: stored until you remove them or delete your account

— P2P transferred files: never stored on our servers

— Account data: stored as long as your account exists

— Session data: expires after 30 days of inactivity

Infrastructure

Stickr runs on the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

— Railway (application hosting and database)

— Cloudflare (R2 storage, TURN relay, DNS, email routing)

— Google (OAuth authentication)

— Dodo Payments (payment processing)

Your rights

You can delete your pinned files and profile data at any time through the app. If you want your account fully deleted, contact us and we'll remove all your data from our database. Cloud-uploaded files are automatically deleted after their expiry period.

Changes to this policy

We'll update this page when the product changes. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach out at privacy@getstickr.in.

Stickr is designed to keep your data private by default. We collect only what's needed and delete what we can.
